# Business PRD (Product Requirements Document)

## Product Name
B.A (Broker Assistant)

---

# Problem

Real estate brokers in Egypt spend a significant amount of time searching across multiple sources such as:

- WhatsApp groups
- Telegram channels
- Facebook groups
- Excel sheets
- Internal company databases

This fragmented workflow causes delays, missed opportunities, duplicate work, and poor client experience.

---

# Target Users

Primary Users:
- Independent Real Estate Brokers
- Brokerage Companies
- Sales Teams

Future Users:
- Property Developers
- Property Owners
- Real Estate Investors

---

# Product Vision

B.A will become the AI Copilot for every real estate broker in Egypt.

Instead of searching manually, brokers simply describe the client's request in natural language, and B.A returns the best matching properties within seconds.

---

# Core Value Proposition

B.A helps brokers:
- Save time
- Close deals faster
- Find better property matches
- Organize their inventory
- Increase productivity

---

# MVP Objective

The first version of B.A will focus on one thing only:

Helping brokers find the best matching property in less than one minute.

Everything else will be added later.
