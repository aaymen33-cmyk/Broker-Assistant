
# User Flow

## Step 1

The broker opens B.A.

↓

## Step 2

The broker types the client's request.

Example:

"I need a 180 sqm apartment in New Cairo under EGP 8 million."

↓

## Step 3

B.A understands the request using AI.

↓

## Step 4

B.A searches all available property databases.

↓

## Step 5

The best matching properties are ranked.

↓

## Step 6

The broker opens a property.

↓

## Step 7

The broker can:

- View details
- View images
- Call owner
- Share property
- Save to favorites
- Add notes

↓

## Step 8

The broker sends the property to the client.

Mission completed.
