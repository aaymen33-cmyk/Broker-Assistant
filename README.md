# B.A (Broker Assistant)

## Vision

B.A is an AI-powered real estate copilot built for the Egyptian market.

Our mission is to help every real estate broker find the right property in less than one minute using Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Problem

Today, brokers spend hours searching through:

- Excel files
- WhatsApp groups
- Telegram channels
- Facebook posts
- Different brokerage companies

This causes delays, lost clients, and missed sales opportunities.

---

## Solution

B.A is an AI assistant that understands natural language.

Instead of manually searching listings, brokers simply describe what the client wants.

Example:

"I need a 180 sqm apartment in New Cairo under EGP 8 million."

B.A understands the request, searches trusted property data, asks follow-up questions if needed, and recommends the best matching properties.

---

## MVP Goal

Help brokers find the best property in under one minute.

Nothing more.

Everything else comes later.

---

## Long-Term Vision

B.A will evolve into the operating system for real estate brokers by combining:

- AI Search
- CRM
- Market Intelligence
- Client Management
- Property Comparison
- Sales Assistant
- AI Voice Assistant
