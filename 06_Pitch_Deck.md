
# B.A (Broker Assistant)

## Elevator Pitch

B.A is an AI-powered real estate copilot designed specifically for the Egyptian market.

It helps brokers find the best matching properties in under one minute using natural language search and artificial intelligence.

---

# The Problem

Real estate brokers waste hours every day searching across:

- WhatsApp groups
- Telegram channels
- Excel sheets
- Facebook groups
- Internal company databases

This leads to:

- Lost opportunities
- Slow response time
- Poor customer experience
- Lower sales

---

# Our Solution

B.A allows brokers to simply type or speak:

"I need a 180 sqm apartment in New Cairo under EGP 8 million."

The AI understands the request, asks follow-up questions if needed, searches available data sources, and recommends the best matching properties instantly.
